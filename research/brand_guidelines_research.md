## Notes
- **Perplexity**: Brand guidelines confirmed to exist and are publicly accessible (referenced in LinkedIn posts, Figma Config 2025 talk, design community coverage), but the direct URL could not be resolved during research. Likely hosted at perplexity.ai/brand or a similar path that returns errors without a browser session.
- **Callaway**: PDF is dated 2019 — may not reflect current brand standards.
- **Fujifilm**: Guidelines are specifically for the X/GFX camera sub-brand, not Fujifilm corporate.
- **Stanley**: This refers to Stanley Black & Decker corporate brand, not the consumer Stanley drinkware brand (Stanley 1913).
- **Amazon**: Guidelines found are specifically for Amazon Advertising partners; no general public brand guide exists.
- **Rivian**: Trademark guidelines list permitted/prohibited uses of the Rivian name and marks; full design system is not public.
- **Kubota**: Logo usage guidelines are from the Kubota Engine division subdomain.
